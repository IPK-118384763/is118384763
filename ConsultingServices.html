Blockchain

I have a huge interest in the code and idea behind blockchain. I believe that blockchain is the future of the internet.

Blockchain is a series of code,that is not stored in one central location but multiple different locations.

How it works is by one party creating a block which is then validated by multiple computers across the net.

This block is then connected to a chain, this chain is the reason that the block can be traced back to its very creation.

It is virtually impossible to make a fake blockchain, because that would mean falsifying

thousands if not millions of blocks in the chain. Bitcoin and many other cryptocurrency use

blockchain technology.

Immersive Experience

An immersive experience is an illusory environment that completely surrounds you so you feel that you are inside and part of that world.

Immersive Experience is associated with technological environments that create a sense of virtual realities and mixed realities.

There are two different versions of Immersive experience are AR and VR.

AR which stands for Augmented Reality it is the blending of computer generated visuals over our real life environments.

VR stands for Virtual Reality it is the use of computers to create a new environments, unlike AR it completely replaces the users environment.
